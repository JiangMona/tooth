Zodiac Signs

There are twelve zodiac signs: Aries, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius and Pisces. The twelve signs are then divided into four elements: fire, earth, air and water. The signs are also divided into three parts of ten days called decan. the first decan would be the first 10 days of your sign period, the next 10 days would be called the second decan, and approximately the last 10 days being the third decan. Decans can help you dive even further into your zodiac personality!
